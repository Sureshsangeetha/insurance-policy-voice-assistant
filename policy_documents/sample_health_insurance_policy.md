## SECTION 1: POLICY COVERAGE

### 1.1 Medical Expenses Covered

This health insurance policy provides coverage for the following medical expenses:

**Hospitalization Coverage:**
- Room rent: Up to $500 per day for standard rooms
- ICU charges: Up to $1,000 per day
- Hospital room charges including boarding expenses
- Nursing expenses as per hospital charges
- Doctor's consultation fees during hospitalization
- Anesthesia, blood, oxygen, operation theater charges
- Surgical procedures and related expenses
- Pre-hospitalization expenses: 60 days before admission
- Post-hospitalization expenses: 90 days after discharge

**Diagnostic Services:**
- Laboratory tests and investigations
- X-rays, CT scans, MRI scans
- Ultrasound and diagnostic imaging
- ECG and other cardiac tests
- Blood tests and pathology services

**Emergency Services:**
- Emergency ambulance charges: Up to $200 per hospitalization
- Emergency room treatment
- Accidental injury treatment
- Emergency surgical procedures

**Day Care Procedures:**
- Dialysis treatment
- Chemotherapy and radiation therapy
- Lithotripsy procedures
- Cataract surgery
- Tonsillectomy
- Other procedures not requiring 24-hour hospitalization

### 1.2 Coverage Amounts

**Individual Coverage Plan:**
- Sum Insured: $100,000 per policy year
- Room rent limit: $500 per day
- ICU limit: $1,000 per day
- Annual deductible: $500

**Family Floater Plan:**
- Sum Insured: $200,000 per policy year (shared among family members)
- Covers: Self, spouse, 2 dependent children (up to 25 years)
- Room rent limit: $500 per day per person
- ICU limit: $1,000 per day per person
- Annual deductible: $1,000 per family

**Coverage Period:**
- Policy Year: January 1 to December 31
- Renewable annually
- Grace period: 30 days for premium payment

- Investigational or experimental treatments

### 4.2 Waiting Periods

**Initial Waiting Period:**
- 30 days from policy start date

**Pre-existing Disease Waiting Period:**
- 3 years for pre-existing conditions

**Specific Disease Waiting Period:**
- 2 years for specific conditions:
  - Hernia
  - Cataract
  - Kidney stones
  - Gall bladder stones
  - Sinusitis

**Maternity Waiting Period:**
- 2 years from policy inception
- Must be continuously covered

## SECTION 5: CLAIM PROCESS

### 5.1 Cashless Claims (Network Hospitals)

**Step-by-Step Process:**

**Step 1: Hospital Admission**
- Present your health insurance card at hospital admission desk
- Provide policy number and ID proof
- Fill out cashless request form

**Step 2: Pre-Authorization**
- Hospital submits pre-authorization request to insurer
- Include: Treatment plan, estimated costs, medical reports
- Submitted electronically through TPA

**Step 3: Approval**
- Emergency cases: Approved within 2-6 hours
- Planned procedures: Approved within 24 hours
- Insurer may request additional information

**Step 4: Treatment**
- Proceed with approved treatment
- Hospital will handle direct billing
- You only pay for non-covered items (if any)

**Step 5: Discharge**
- Final bill processed by hospital and insurer
- Pay any deductible or co-payment
- Collect discharge summary

### 5.2 Reimbursement Claims (Non-Network Hospitals)

**Step 1: Pay Hospital Bills**
- Make full payment to hospital
- Collect all original bills and receipts
- Get detailed discharge summary

**Step 2: Document Collection**
Required documents:
- Duly filled claim form (signed)
- Original hospital bills and receipts
- Discharge summary
- Doctor's prescriptions
- Diagnostic test reports
- Investigation reports
- Pharmacy bills
- Admission note
- ID proof and policy document

**Step 3: Submit Claim**
- Submit within 30 days of discharge
- Submit to: claims@sampleinsurance.com
- Or mail to registered office
- Or upload on mobile app/website

**Step 4: Claim Processing**
- Acknowledgment within 3 working days
- Processing time: 15-30 days
- May request additional information

**Step 5: Settlement**
- Approved amount credited to bank account
- SMS and email notification sent
- Settlement letter provided

### 5.3 Emergency Claims

**For Emergency Hospitalization:**
- No pre-authorization needed for emergency
- Get admitted immediately
- Inform insurer within 24 hours of admission
- Call helpline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX (24/7)
- Provide: Policy number, hospital name, condition

**Emergency Contact:**
- Toll-free: 1-800-XXX-XXXX
- Email: emergency@sampleinsurance.com

## SECTION 10: DEFINITIONS

**Hospitalization:** Admission to a hospital for at least 24 hours for medical treatment as an inpatient.

**Day Care Procedure:** Medical treatment taken in less than 24 hours due to technological advancement.

**Pre-existing Disease:** Any condition, ailment, or injury that existed before policy inception.

**Network Hospital:** Hospitals with cashless facility arrangement with the insurer.

**Sum Insured:** Maximum amount payable under the policy in a policy year.

**Policy Year:** 12 months from the policy start date.

**Waiting Period:** Time period during which specific conditions are not covered.

**Deductible:** Amount to be paid by policyholder before insurance coverage begins.

**Co-payment:** Percentage of claim amount to be paid by policyholder.
